The Best Movies According to Simon

My top 3 movies of all time.

  1. Interstellar

    IMDb Link

    Interstellar is a remarkable sci-fi film that combines stunning visuals, emotional depth, and complex scientific concepts to explore humanity's survival, love, and the mysteries of space and time.

  2. The 13th Warrior

    IMDb Link

    The 13th Warrior is great for its intense, atmospheric blend of Viking legend and action, where cultural clashes and thrilling battles create a unique and memorable adventure.

  3. Kingdom of Heaven

    IMDb Link

    Kingdom of Heaven is a visually stunning historical epic that combines powerful storytelling, rich character development, and thought-provoking themes on faith and tolerance, making it a must-watch for fans of historical dramas.
